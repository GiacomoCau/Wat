35. Syntetic Expressions

  • Idea stolen from Anarki https://github.com/arclanguage/anarki :-)

%´

  • ´a;b -> (compose a b c)
    ´a;b;c -> (compose* a b c)
    ´!a -> (compose ! a)
    ´a…b -> (a b)
    ´a…'b -> (a 'b)
    ´a…'b…c -> (a 'b c)
    ´a,b -> (peval a b)
    ´a,'b,c -> (peval* a 'b c)
    ´a,_,'b,c -> (\ (_) (a _ 'b c))
    ´dd,_c,1,_b,"a",_a,_a,'c,_,:ff,_* -> (\ (_ _a _b _c . _*) (dd _c 1 _b "a" _a _a 'c _ :ff _*))
    ´(dd _c 1 _b "a" _a _a 'c _ :ff _*) -> (\ (_ _a _b _c . _*) (dd _c 1 _b "a" _a _a 'c _ :ff _*))
